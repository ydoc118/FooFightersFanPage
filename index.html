Biography

The Foo Fighters are an American rock bank that was formed in 1994 by Dave Grohl. Dave Grohl was the former Nirvana drummer and started
Foo Fighters as a one man band after Kurt Cobain's death. The name "Foo Fighters" came from reportings of UFO's and other aerial phenomena
reported during WWII, these were known as the "Foo Fighters".

The first album released was a self titled album, "Foo Fighters" and released in 1995, they began touring the same year. There would be multiple
changes to band members early on, but would eventually find the right members to continue with for years to come.

Since their first album release in 1995, the Foo Fighters have released nine albums and continue to tour and create new music to this day.

To keep up with their new releases and check their tour dates go to: FooFighters.com
